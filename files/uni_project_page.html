university of northern iowa

print and digital graphics

Since August 2023, I have worked as a graphic design assistant for the College of Humanities, Arts, and Sciences at the University of Northern Iowa while working towards my degree. I worked on a variety of different projects, promoting different events and programs at the university. This included various print and digital graphics, both within and outside of the visual identity of UNI.

While I worked on many projects collaboratively with both my superiors and other student employees, these projects are all ones I personally executed from start to finish.
