Store

Get a preview of what you can expect from Cup of Joe's unique storefront. Spoiler, it's not just coffee!

Our Roasts

Roasts are rotated every 3 months to keep your tongue on its tip-toes, and our celebrated flavors make any day special. This includes our famous Highlander Grog, Blueberry Cinnamon Swirl, and Chocolate Almond Junkie.

Signature Drinks

With choices including the Screaming Chicken latte, Grandma's Cake Batter breve, the Rock Star mocha, and many others, we'll sure you'll find something you'll enjoy. We invite you to recommend your own flavor combos to ensure your taste-buds leave satisfied.

Not Just Joe

If tea is your fancy, peruse our extensive variety of Republic of Tea tea flavors. Anything from red, black, black blended to white or green, we have it. Enjoy a cup here or take a tin home to enjoy at your leisure. We also offer a variety of other non-coffee beverages.

Bakery Items

Cup of Joe's in-house bakers provide a wide selection of sweet and savory baked goods every day, including vegan and wheat-free options.

Merchandise

If you love our mascot as much as we do, stop in and buy a mug, sweatshirt, hat, or other item with their adorable face on it. And if you're looking to replicate the Cup of Joe coffee experience, you can take home a bag of our coffee beans.
