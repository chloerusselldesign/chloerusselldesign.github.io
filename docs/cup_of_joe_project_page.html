COFFEE THAT HITS THE SPOT

Cup of Joe is a local coffee shop in Cedar Falls, Iowa with a quirky 1950s aesthetic and a dedication to ethically sourced coffee. Filled with midcentury furniture and works by local artists, it's unlike any other coffee shop in the area. They need a visual identity that is colorful, unifying, and has plenty of personality.
