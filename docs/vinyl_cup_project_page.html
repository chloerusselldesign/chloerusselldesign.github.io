Not Like Other Record Shops

Vinyl Cup in Cedar Falls, Iowa offers more than just a wide selection of new and used vinyl. With complimentary beverages and excellent customer service, Vinyl Cup is unlike any other record shop. Or you can shop online, which is cool, too.

A Rebrand to Remember

As a brand, Vinyl Cup has no shortage of personality. However, their current website leaves a lot to be desired. The goal of this redesign is to breathe life into the brand, consolidate webpages in a sensical way, and streamline online shopping.
