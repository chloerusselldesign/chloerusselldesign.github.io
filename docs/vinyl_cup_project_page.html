hallowtyde

brand concept and website design - 2023

HallowTyde is a brand concept for a festival revolving around historical Halloween celebrations. HallowTyde is where a modern pumpkin patch meets the renaissance faire for fun for all ages.

HallowTyde's branding embraces a cute aesthetic with quirky illustrations, catering to a family-oriented audience. The project includes logos, website designs, and various print promotional materials.
