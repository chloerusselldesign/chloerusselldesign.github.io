vinyl cup

brand concept and website design - 2023

Vinyl Cup in Cedar Falls, IA is a record shop that prides itself on customer service. They welcome everyone, whether they're avid vinyl collectors or don't own a single record. They offer every customer a drink and a chance to talk about their favorite music.

The goal of this website redesign was to simplify the menu and make it easier for customers to find the information or records they're looking for. While Vinyl Cup has a strong logo and brand identity, it was not adequately distilled into a visual identity on the website. The new design hones in on the vintage aesthetic and is much more user-friendly.
